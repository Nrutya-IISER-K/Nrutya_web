About Us

The Dance Club exists to celebrate the joy and power of dance by providing an inclusive space for students of all skill levels. It encourages exploration of diverse styles, creative expression, and meaningful connections among members.

Through workshops, rehearsals, performances, and social events, the club fosters a supportive environment where individuals develop their skills, confidence, artistic voice, while sharing their passion with the wider college community. Dance, for us, is expression, joy, and a means of building lasting friendships.

Our Flagship Events

Garba Night:

Nrutya organises the Garba Night around the month of October, celebrating the vibrant spirit of Navratri and promoting cultural appreciation among students. Open to all students with a nominal entry fee, the event features an evening of enthusiastic Garba dancing in a lively and inclusive atmosphere. Participants embrace the festive spirit by wearing traditional attire and dancing together to Garba music. Food and beverage stalls further enhanced the experience. Overall, Garba Night, introduced in 2024 remains a highly successful event that brings the student community together through tradition, music, and celebration.

Workshops:

Classical Workshop:

This workshop provides participants with an opportunity to learn semi-classical dance under the guidance of eminent dancers with years of experience in this genre. This generally remains a 4-day workshop spanning over August and September. 15+ participants learn choreographed performances on a minimum of two songs, which become an important part of the outer fest dance presentation. The workshop remains highly successful.

Hip-Hop Dance Workshop:

This workshop pioneered the introduction of Hip-Hop dance at IISER Kolkata. Highly accomplished dancers and choreographers teaches the participants in the workshop with fully choreographed routines on atleast two songs. With 25+ participants, it is one of the largest and most successful workshops organized by Nrutya, and parts of the choreography were incorporated into both the IICM Group Dance and the outer fest dance performances.

Interbatch Dance Competition:
Nrutya has been successfully organising the Inter-Batch Dance Competition in the Spring Semester, providing students with a platform to showcase their talent, creativity, and teamwork. The event features duet and group categories, with enthusiastic participation from multiple batches and well-executed performances within the prescribed guidelines. The competition is judged by a distinguished panel comprising of renowned national level dancers.Marked by impressive performances, strong team spirit, and smooth coordination, the event remains a grand success that celebrates dance and strengthens the cultural vibrancy of the IISER Kolkata community.

Rules and Regulations of the Club :

Booking the Room:

  • Booking opens daily at 12 AM via the official WhatsApp group (mention time slot).
  • Maximum booking: 2 hours per day (continuous or split).
  • OBs may pre-allocate the room up to 1 week before cultural events/Nrutya productions (on prior request by the cultural team).
  • For event bookings, email: dance.activity@iiserkol.ac.in
  • In special cases (multiple events/workshops), pre-booking may extend to 10 days prior.Otherwise, follow regular booking procedures.

Use of Electronics:

  • The room has 2 speakers with remotes.
  • Handle all electronics carefully; damages will incur fines.
  • Switch off fans, lights, TV, speakers, and AC after use.
  • Return all remotes to the SAC Security Desk.

Acessing Room & Equiqment:

  • Sign the “Keys”register to collect room keys and “Items” register for remotes.
  • Lock the room and return keys after use.
  • For club items (e.g., knee caps, ghungroos), contact OBs directly.

General Instructions:

  • Room usage allowed until 12 AM unless special permission is granted by DoSA.
  • Shoes must be removed; maintain cleanliness at all times.
  • Do not tamper with or unnecessarily move items.

Cleanliness Maintenance:

  • Dispose of trash promptly.
  • Switch off all appliances.
  • Avoid entering with dirty shoes.
  • Leave the room in impeccable condition.
  • Dance-specific shoes are allowed if strictly not used outdoors.

Penalties:

  • Rule violations → 1-week booking ban.
  • Damage to property → compensation + possible suspension.
  • OBs will conduct random inspections.
  • Poor cleanliness → suspension until improvement is shown.
  • Repeated violations → revoked access, possible escalation to SAC Cultural Secretary/DoSA.
